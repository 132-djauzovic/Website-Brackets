Well im gonna show you how to do that do.To be able to insert a video you have to encode it, thats what Adobe media encoder is for, i will give you the optimal settings you can use that will work the best.

  • Resolution: 720x480
  • 30fps
  • Aspect - Square Pixels

  • Target Bitrate - 1.5
  • Max Bitrate 1.8
  • Use Max Render
  • Audio AAC
  • Sample Rate 44100
  • Mono Audio
  • Bitrate 80
